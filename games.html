Just Go That Way

It shouldn't be that hard.

Just Go That Way is my most recent game which I worked on for roughly a month. In Just Go That Way I attempted a very simplistic design with fluent animations and tried to make a very simple concept as elegant as possible. I spent a long time on the design of the game, as the concept was rather simple.

released on 7/7/16

multitaskilus

Don't relieve stress, create it!

Multitaskilus is a game I spent around a month on in which the player is presented with many tasks and little time to do them. Multitaskilus became rather popular on reddit, resulting in 1300 page views, making it my most popular game.

released on 2/4/16

sqar

A puzzling game with squares.

sqar was the second major game I created in which I tried various experimental design components, including material-design shadows and audio. Though it's still incomplete and playing past level 50 will crash the game, it's still fun to look at and play.

Jhumpa Simulator

A physcoanalytic LA project.

For an LA project on a choice book, in which I chose Jhumpa Lahiri's Intepreter of Maladies, I decided to create a video game for my LA project. In the video game, which is confusing out of context, the player takes over Jhumpa's mind as she writes her book. Though it's not incredibly fun, it was my first time experimenting with animating static images and learning CSS3 animations.

released on 4/28/16
